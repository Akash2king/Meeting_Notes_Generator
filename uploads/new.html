TOURNAMENT RULES & REGULATIONS

Team Composition

• Teams must consist of 4 players with 1 substitute player (optional)

• All players must be at least 16 years old

• Players must use their own registered Free Fire account

Match Format

• Battle Royale - Squad Mode

• Points System: Kills (1 point each) + Position Points

• Top 12 teams advance to finals

Tournament Stages

• Qualifiers: Online (Best of 3)

• Quarter Finals: Online (Best of 5)

• Semi Finals: LAN Event (Best of 5)

• Finals: LAN Event (Best of 7)

Fair Play

• Use of hacks, mods, or cheats will result in immediate disqualification

• All players must use official Free Fire app

• Screen recording is mandatory during online matches
